Bakugo Katsuki

Bio

Bakugo Katsuki is a student hero in training in classroom 1A at the prestigious school called UA in Japan. His mission is simple and straightforward; to become the number one hero. His quirk is labeled explosion. It allows him to secrete nitroglycerin-like sweat from the palms of his hands and also the ability to ignite it on command to create explosions.

Bakugo in his hero costume.

Characteristics of Bakugo

  1. He's not the most friendly in class 1A.
  2. He does however give repect to those he feels deserve it. Including All Might, Todoroki's sister Fuyumi, and Ochako Uraraka to name a few.
  3. He is a major hot head and calls people names he creates rather than their given or even family name.
  4. He only has a few people he'd consider his friends but is not very likely to admit it to their faces.
